I'm a junior at Wellesley College majoring in Media Arts and Sciences and minoring in Economics, with a passion for interactive media and its application. I'm interested in exploring user understanding, product development, and marketing.

In my recent project titled "The Good Place," I delved into the nuanced emotional struggles faced by students at Wellesley College, aiming to understand and address their unique needs. Through comprehensive user analysis, I gained valuable insights into their daily experiences and the specific challenges they encounter. Leveraging this knowledge, I played an integral role in the design of an innovative app tailored to provide support and resources for these students. The app serves as a digital companion, offering a safe space for students to express their emotions, access mental health resources, and connect with a supportive community. My commitment to this project reflects my passion for enhancing student well-being and my belief in the power of technology to create positive change in educational environments

Beyond academic, my interests extend to movie, TV show, game, and various of media entertainments.
