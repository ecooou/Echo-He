Bookcovers

This project was created during my studies in Denmark to create a mini-book series for Nordic literature. The project creates three pocket-sized book covers and two posters incorporating elements from the story to construct a cohesive visual identity. It explores the reimagining and rearrangement of these elements to achieve a striking visual impact, even within the constraints of a small format.
